Mission

We work hard every day to support children and young adults with disability and their families.

History

The centre first opened in 1973 in the buildings of a school dating all the way back to 1865. In 1971 when the original school moved to new and bigger fascilities, the old school was renovated and customized to meet the needs of the care centre. From then on, the centre has grown and expanded into new locations serving a larger geographical area and providing an even wider array of specialized services.
