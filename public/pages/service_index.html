Library

The library lends out books for children and young adults, as well as special pedagogical literature for kindergartens, schools and parents.

Swimming

Swimming is a good form of exercising for people having trouble moving. We offer both indvidual exercising and group hours.

Speech Therapy

Children and young adults can get help with adjusting theirs speech sound articulation to meet the expected normal pronunciation. Also people having problems with stuttering can get help to speak more fluently.

Psychotherapy

Treatment of children with mental disorders differs from that of treating older people. At the center we have the specific knowledge required to carefully treat children and young adults.

Assistant Service

Our assistants can help in school and ease the workload of the family in the spare time. By utilizing an assistant, some children that would have needed to go to a specially adapted school can attend a normal school on the same level as other children.

Education

Children and young adults that are not able to attend normal schools can utilize our services. Our teachers all have education and experience in teaching children that have problems learning in the conventional school system.
