Description

The library lends out books for children and young adults, as well as special pedagogical literature for kindergartens, schools and parents.

By offering a great variety of easy to read books, picture books and textbooks there is something for everyone at the library. Furthermore, a section of the library is dedicated to scientific books, covering topics such as children psychology, medical syndroms and other special pedagogical literature.
